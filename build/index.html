Founded only on Dec. 29, 1847, Atlanta's fairly young.

More than 100 years ago, Atlanta's suburbs expanded from Downtown first toward X, Y, Z. Dozens of other suburbs followed, generally along streetcar lines, then streets for cars. .... More recently, some big new developments have come back Downtown.

Enough buildings and property tax records exist to show when and where Atlanta grew — and has been rebuilt. This map uses building footprints, combined with tax records about the land parcels on which buildings sit, to show those trends.
