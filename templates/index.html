Founded only on Dec. 29, 1847, Atlanta's fairly young.

[DRAFT TEXT TBC] More than 100 years ago, Atlanta's suburbs expanded from Downtown first toward Inman Park and West End. English Avenue, Vine City, Buckhead and dozens of other suburbs followed, generally along streetcar lines, then streets for cars.

More than enough older buildings — and records — exist to show the direction of that growth. Tax records, while imperfect, do often include a building's construction year, which is mapped here to show the broad outline of the city's history.
