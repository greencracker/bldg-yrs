DeKalb construction year data comes from the county tax commissioner's office. Using mapping software, DeKalb buildnings were assigned a construction year based on tax information tied to the parcel on which they sit. In the very few cases of several buildings all on one parcel (like the East Lake Golf Club), each building gets assigned the same year, the year of the oldest building on the parcel.

Additional construction year data comes from Atlanta's landmark and historic building registry and the New Georiga Encyclopedia.

The building footprint map, minus small buildings, is publicly available from SR via CARTO.
